Gerber files description:
gtl = top metal layer 
gbl = bottom metal layer
gto = top overlay silkscreen
gtp = top solder paste
gbp = bottom solder paste
gts = top solder mask
gbs = bottom solder mask
gbo = bottom overlay silkscreen
gko = keep out layer
gm1 = individual board outlines
g1 = score lines
g2 = routing lines (overall board outline)
